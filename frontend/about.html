Mission

MediScan helps users make price-aware medicine choices with more confidence.

This site is built as a lightweight e-pharmacy comparison experience. It checks multiple pharmacy platforms, organizes the results into a readable price board, and uses AI to clean naming differences between listings that may refer to the same composition.

The goal is simple: reduce friction, reduce guesswork, and help people compare options faster before making a purchase decision.

Why this site exists

Medicine names vary by brand, packaging, and spelling. MediScan brings them closer together so the comparison feels useful instead of noisy.

Who it is for

Patients, caregivers, pharmacy shoppers, and anyone checking price differences across common Indian e-pharmacy platforms.

Important note

Substitute recommendations should support decision-making, not replace medical advice. Always verify suitability with a qualified professional.
