4th Page

From search term to final comparison, here is the flow behind MEDICOMP+.

Step 1

Search request

A medicine name is submitted from the portal to the FastAPI backend using a simple fetch request.

Step 2

Parallel pharmacy lookups

The backend concurrently requests results from Apollo Pharmacy, Tata 1mg, and PharmEasy.

Step 3

Result cleanup

Scraped names, prices, quantity labels, and manufacturer details are normalized into a shared structure.

Step 4

AI insights

Gemini analyzes the combined listings, estimates the likely salt composition, and suggests equivalent lower-cost substitutes when possible.
